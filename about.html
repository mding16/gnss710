Welcome!

"Invisible Bodies: Hidden Labor Behind Word Embeddings" is an interactive web exhibit that explores the various forms of unseen labor behind the creation and (gender) debiasing of word embedding models like Word2Vec and GloVe. As word embeddings form the foundation of language models, the exhibit also acts as a broader exploration of unseen labor behind modern AI technology.

Please stay a while. Refresh. Select. Click. Move. Hover. There is no wrong way to interact with the site. (And don't be afraid of the glitch.)
