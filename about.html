Welcome!

"Invisible Bodies: Hidden Labor Behind Word Embeddings" is an interactive web exhibit that explores the various forms of hidden labor behind the creation & (gender) debiasing of word embedding models like Word2Vec and GloVe that form the foundations of language models.

Please stay a while. Refresh. Select. Click. Move. Hover. There is no wrong way to interact with the site. (And don't be afraid of the glitch.)
